Animal Info

Hello, I hope that you are doing well! This website is dedicated to current/prospective pet owners. This page will provide fun info and resources relevant to your furry (or scaly) friends.

Are you a prospective pet owner interested in buying a pet? In that case, check out the Information page. There, you will find general information about common pets (specifically, dogs, cats, goldfish, and hamsters) to compare and contrast, so that you can find the right animal for you!

If you're interested in finding the best food to buy for your current pet, we have links to awesome food products you can buy. Check out the food page!

Lastly, we also allow you the opportunity to treat your friend to a treat on their special day! If it's your pet's birthday, go to the birthday page, enter some information about your pet, and you will get a personalized message wishing your pet a happy birthday!
